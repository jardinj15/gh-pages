1900-1939

The most important technologic discovery

The most important technological discovery in the era of 1900-1939 would be the airplane. I selected this technology as the most important because it changed the world in many ways including military and civilian usage. The airplane is used every day by many people in order to travel from point A to point B faster than by boat.

The person(s) responsible for brining this technology to the public

The Wright brothers are responsible for bringing this technology to the public. The two brothers stared in a bike shop and then moved into aircraft starting with a glider and then adding control and finally power in order to create an airplane as we know today.

How has this technology impacted society?

The airplane his impacted society in many ways because it had made connecting the world much easier. If aircraft had not been invented we would only be using boats to travel across the ocean for any type of travel.
